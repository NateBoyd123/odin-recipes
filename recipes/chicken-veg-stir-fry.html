Chicken and Vegetable Stir Fry

Description

Chicken and Vegetable Stir Fry is a classic Asian-inspired dish that is beloved for its simple yet delicious flavors and ease of preparation. The dish typically consists of thinly sliced chicken breast that is stir-fried with a variety of colorful vegetables, such as bell peppers, onions, and broccoli, and seasoned with a sweet and savory sauce made from soy sauce, honey, garlic, and ginger. The key to a good stir fry is to cook everything quickly over high heat, which creates a nice contrast in textures between the tender, juicy chicken and the crisp-tender vegetables.

One of the best things about Chicken and Vegetable Stir Fry is its versatility. You can customize the dish to suit your taste preferences and dietary needs by using your favorite vegetables, adjusting the amount of sauce or spice, or even swapping out the protein for tofu or beef. It's also a great way to use up any leftover vegetables you have in the fridge. The dish is not only delicious and satisfying, but it's also healthy and nutritious, as it's packed with protein, fiber, and a variety of vitamins and minerals from the vegetables. Overall, Chicken and Vegetable Stir Fry is a flavorful and easy-to-make dish that is perfect for a quick and healthy weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, onions, and broccoli), chopped into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat.
  2. Add the sliced chicken and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until it is cooked through and no longer pink.
  3. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.
  4. Add the mixed vegetables to the skillet and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they are crisp-tender.
  5. Add the minced garlic, grated ginger, and red pepper flakes (if using) to the skillet and stir-fry for an additional 30 seconds to 1 minute until fragrant.
  6. Return the chicken to the skillet and stir to combine with the vegetables.
  7. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce and honey.
  8. Pour the soy sauce mixture over the chicken and vegetables and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until the sauce is heated through and evenly distributed.
  9. Remove the skillet from the heat and serve the stir-fry hot with rice or noodles, if desired.
